Common Questions

How does it work?

Step One

Choose your preferred location, date, and time for your rental delivery.

Step Two

Place a 50% deposit to secure your booking.

Step Three

On the day of your rental, we’ll arrive at your chosen location. Before setup, we require the remaining balance plus a $50 refundable deposit. This deposit will be returned after the rental is completed, provided nothing is missing, damaged, or stolen.

Step Four

We’ll take care of setting everything up for you.

Step Five

Enjoy your rental experience! If you finish early, feel free to call or text us and we’ll come clean up.

Step Six

No need to worry about the mess, we’ll handle the cleanup!

Will I get exactly what’s shown in the photos?

We do our best to match the photos, but we can’t guarantee every item will be included. Occasionally, pieces may be unavailable due to damage or theft. If there’s a specific item you’d like to ensure is part of your rental, please reach out! We’ll do our best to accommodate your request.

How far will you travel?

We’re happy to travel anywhere in New England. Depending on your location, a travel fee of up to $150 may apply.

Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees here! We’re fully transparent about pricing. In addition
to your rental fee, we require a $50 refundable deposit to cover potential
damage or missing items. Travel fees may also apply, and all costs will be clearly communicated before booking.

Is food included?

Due to varying state and local laws, we are unable to provide food of any kind.
