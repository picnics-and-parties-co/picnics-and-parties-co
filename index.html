About Picnics and Parties Co.

At Picnics and Parties Co., we specialize in creating elegant, portable tea parties and styled picnics — perfect for any special occasion.

Whether you're planning a bachelorette party, bridal shower, birthday celebration, proposal, date night, dinner party, or any intimate gathering, we bring the charm and sophistication directly to you. Our events blend the beauty of antique and modern fine china, each piece thoughtfully selected to create a colorful, curated atmosphere that feels both luxurious and inviting.

To help capture the magic, we also offer event photography as an optional add-on. For an additional $50–$300, you'll receive beautifully composed and edited images taken with a professional-grade camera. While this service does not include high-end retouching or Photoshop, it’s a perfect, budget-friendly way to preserve the memories of your special day with stunning, high-quality photos.

Let Picnics and Parties Co. help you celebrate in style — effortlessly.
